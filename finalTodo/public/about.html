About Us

Toodify is a modern, secure, and user-friendly todo management app built for everyone who wants to organize their daily tasks efficiently. Our mission is to help you boost your productivity by providing a simple interface and powerful features like secure signup/login, easy task creation, editing, and deletion.

Whether you are a student, professional, or homemaker, Toodify is designed to make your life easier. Your data is safe with us, and you can access your todos from anywhere, anytime.

  • ✔️ Fast and secure authentication
  • ✔️ Clean and responsive design
  • ✔️ All CRUD operations for your todos
  • ✔️ Built with modern technologies (Node.js, MongoDB, Express, Zod, JWT, bcrypt)
